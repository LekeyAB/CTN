Founder

Terrence Taylor is an African Nova Scotian artist & entrepreneur who grew up in Las Vegas, Nevada. This unique & dualistic experience shaped a default perspective of viewing and mashing together things that may not be meant to be. And yet, magic consistently happens. Since 2017, Terrence has combined storytelling elements from hip hop, Hollywood, radio and the Internet into a style coined Innovative Narrative, selling nearly $1,000,000 in video and podcast content to corporate, grassroots and government clients across Canada.
